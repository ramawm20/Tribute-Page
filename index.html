Here's a time line of Albert Einstein's life:

  • 1879 -Born in Ulm, Germany
  • 1894 -The family moved to Italy in search of work and Einstein - aged 15 - remained in Munich to complete his studies at Luitpold Gymnasium
  • 1895 -He entered the Argovian Cantonal Gymnasium in Aarau-Switzerland to complete his secondary education. While living with the family of Joost Winteler, he fell in love with Winteler's daughter, Marek.Then With his father's approval, Einstein renounced his citizenship in the German Kingdom of Württemberg to avoid military service.
  • 1900 -Einstein's paper ("Deductions from the Filament Phenomenon") was published in the Annals of Physics
  • 1901 -Obtained Swiss citizenship and his's first paper on capillary attraction was published under the title "Folgerungen aus den Capillaritätserscheinungen"
  • 1903 -Einstein and Marek married in January
  • 1905 -It dubbed The Miracle Year He received his PhD from the University of Zurich and also he published four groundbreaking papers on the photoelectric effect, Brownian motion, special relativity, and mass-energy equivalence.
  • 1908 -The research recognized Einstein as a leading scientist and he was appointed as a lecturer at the University of Bern.
  • 1911 -Einstein became a full professor at the German Charles Ferdinand University in Prague , and agreed to take Austrian citizenship in the Austro-Hungarian Empire to do so.And he published another article under the title "On the effect of gravity on the propagation of light."
  • 1914 -He was appointed director of the Kaiser Wilhelm Physical Institute and professor at the University of Berlin.
  • 1916 -Einstein was elected president of the German Physical Society and he predicted the existence of gravitational waves
  • 1919 -Einstein and Marek separated after living separately for five years, and as part of the divorce settlement, Einstein agreed to give Maric - in the future - the Nobel Prize money, after which Einstein married Elsa, his cousin.
  • 1920 -He became a foreign member of the Royal Dutch Academy of Arts and Sciences.
  • 1921 -Einstein won the Nobel Prize in Physics, was elected a foreign member of the Royal Society, and published an article entitled “My First Impressions of the United States of America.”
  • 1925 -Received the Copley Medal from the Royal Society, Einstein visited South America, and Einstein was a figurehead in helping establish the Hebrew University of Jerusalem, which opened this year.
  • 1930 -Einstein and his former student Leo Szilard co-invented and patented the Einstein refrigerator.
  • 1935 -Einstein collaborated with Nathan Rosen to produce a model of the wormhole that is often called the Einstein-Rosen Bridge.
  • 1946 -Einstein visited Lincoln University in Pennsylvania.
  • 1949 -He received an honorary doctorate from the Hebrew University of Jerusalem.
  • 1954 -A year before his death, Einstein told his old friend, Linus Pauling, “I made a great mistake in my life, when I signed the letter to President Roosevelt recommending the building of atomic bombs; But there was some justification, the danger that the Germans might make them...».
  • 1955 -Dies at the age of 76
